Privacy Policy

Little Tracer (ABC Kids Learning App)
Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

This app is designed for children and is listed in the Apple App Store Kids Category. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information from children or parents.

Data We Store

All learning progress, achievements, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that receive child data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or email collection
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No third-party ad or tracking SDKs

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and payments are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment card information.

Parent Rights

Parents can export or delete all locally stored data from Parent Dashboard → Privacy & Data inside the app.
